Education

STEM High School for Boys - 6th of October

Have been selected among the brightest 175 Egyptian students in the country to attend this innovative boarding school with a merit scholarship. I am working on a separate capstone project every semester to solve one of Egypt's grand challenges in addition to studying a STEM-focused Honors curriculum.

Stanford University

Studied Number Theory and Cryptography as a part of Stanford Pre-Collegiate Summer Institutes for 2021 summer after being selected among an extremely competitive pool of applicants to attend the program with a full scholarship.

MIT LaunchX

Selected among a very competitive pool of applicants to receive a semi-full scholarship to attend the program. I have studied Entrepreneurship, in addition to teaming up with other international students to launch Parkingdom.
"A company developing parking guidance systems with enhanced features and low cost."
Also, I have got the "biggest risk-taker" title for the 2021 class and was chosen to be a brand ambassador!

X-Culture Academy

Selected among a competitive pool of applicants to attend X-Culture Academy with a semi-full scholarship. I have worked in an international team with peers from around the world to solve a real-life problem presented by a real-life company called Finnish Schools International. I have learned about other countries cultures and how to collaborate online and work in international teams in addition to studying an MPA-based curriculum.
